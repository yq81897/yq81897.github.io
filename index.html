Introduce Myself

Hi everyone, my name is Sugar, a girl who is 6 months old.
I am Domestic Medium Hair. Also, I have mix Black and Brown furs.
I was born in May 28th,2018. But I was abandoned by the last owner when I was only two months old...
I was abandoned on a highway, where vehicles driving very fast.
I felt so scared and hungry.
I wanted to find some food, however,it was hard for me, a 6-months-old girl to move on the highway.
Such days lasted for several days.

... ...

Luckily! There was some one found me on the highway and took me to the Oconee Country Animal Shelter!!
I got food, clean water and treatment there. More important thins was, I felt people's love there.
My new owner appeared in front of me at August 19th,2018.
I saw her through the glasses. She looked so nice. She gently knocked on the glasses in order to attract my attention.
I felt nervous but excited. Maybe I am able to have my own family like other cats.
The girl, she finally decided to take me back to her home and gave me a new name in Chinese, which is JiangYou.
Umm..Actually I did not know Chinese but I guess maybe it is same as the English word "soy sauce".
Maybe it is because of my furs color...?
Whatever, I finally have my own family with three lovely girls, and my new life began!
